## Owns

- router-to-agent front-door operations;
- root `Operation` / `Reply` / `Event` generated by `signal_channel!`;
- `AgentIdentifier`, `AgentBackend`, `DeliveryToken`, delivery records,
  transcript stream records, observation records, and skeleton-unimplemented
  records;
- round-trip tests for the ordinary agent surface.

## Does not own

- owner-only agent policy or spawn verbs;
- `persona-agent-daemon` actors, storage, backend registry, or child process
  supervision;
- backend-private contracts such as future `signal-persona-claude`;
- router delivery durability state.

## Invariants

- Router talks to one agent socket and multiplexes by `AgentIdentifier`.
- Backend choice is observed here but assigned by owner policy elsewhere.
- `Tap` and `Untap` are macro-injected observability operations, not domain
  delivery verbs.
- Wire enums are closed and every public variant has a round-trip witness.
